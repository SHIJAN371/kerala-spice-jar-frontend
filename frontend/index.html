Our Story

A kitchen full of memories in every jar

Kerala Spice Jar began at my mother's kitchen table in Kumarakom, where the air was always thick with the perfume of fresh curry leaves and cracked mustard seeds. Every recipe here has been passed down through generations — not written, but tasted, remembered, and perfected.

We use no preservatives, no artificial colours — just the finest local ingredients sourced from Kerala's spice gardens and coastal farms.
